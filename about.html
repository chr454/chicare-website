About Chicare Ventures

Our Story

At Chicare Ventures, We are passionate about bringing the best in natural beauty, delicious treats and vibrant fashion. Founded in 2021 by CEO Nwamata Rita Chioma, our journey began with a mission to provide high quality natural glowing products and perfumes. Over time, we've expanded our offerings to include handmade wears, and crunchy chinchin.

Our Goal

Our goal is to become a leading provider of natural beauty products, tasty treats, and stylish fashion, while building long-lasting relationships with our customers and community
